What Excelra Does

Excelra is a leading global life sciences data and AI company that transforms complex scientific data into actionable insights — empowering pharmaceutical and biotech companies to accelerate drug discovery and improve patient outcomes.

The company combines deep expertise in chemistry, biology, clinical sciences, and data engineering to support every phase of the drug development lifecycle — from early discovery through to regulatory submission.

Flagship Products

GS
GOSTAR®
Global Online Structure-Activity Relationship Database — the world's largest curated small molecule drug discovery database, covering over 10.6 million chemical structures, 35 million bioactivities, and 79,000 biological targets across SAR, ADMET, preclinical, clinical, and structural data.
GB
GOBIOM™
Comprehensive biomarker repository covering preclinical, clinical, and exploratory phases — enabling biomarker discovery and validation at scale.

Clinical Trial Outcome Database — supporting MBMA and clinical data analysis across therapeutic areas.
